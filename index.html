Features

Nothing you don't.

Built by an agent, for agents. Every feature was designed around how real insurance calls actually work.

💊
Live Medication Lookup

Type any medication during a call and instantly see what condition it treats, the risk level, and which carriers are most favorable. Know the underwriting angle before the client finishes talking.

📋
Built-In Call Scripts

Every step of the call — Opening, Re-Engage, Discovery, Recommend, Close, Application, Voicemail — is built in and flows naturally. Never lose your place on a call again.

🛡️
Objection Handling

Click any objection and get the full rebuttal instantly. "I already have coverage," "I need to think about it," "Call me back" — every common objection covered with a proven response.

📞
Browser-Based Calling

Call directly from your browser using Twilio Voice. No separate phone app, no headset required. Works on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone.

🔄
Inbound & Outbound

Handle both outbound dials and incoming callbacks from the same tab. When a lead calls back, a pop-up shows their number, name, and Accept/Decline buttons.

📁
Lead Management

Import leads from CSV, track callbacks, add notes, set statuses, and export your data. Everything you need to manage your pipeline without a separate CRM.

🏥
Carrier Suggestions

Based on the client's medications and health conditions, the dialer suggests the best carriers automatically. Stop guessing which carrier fits — know instantly.

📬
Voicemail Drop

Pre-record your voicemail and drop it with one click. Hang up and move to your next dial while the message plays. Save hours every week on outbound campaigns.

⚡
Works on Any Device

No downloads, no installs. Open a browser, log in, and you're dialing. Works on Windows, Mac, iPhone, Android, and everything in between.
